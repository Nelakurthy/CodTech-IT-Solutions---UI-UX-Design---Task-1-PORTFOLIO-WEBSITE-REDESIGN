Technologies include CNNs for accurate recognition and IoT devices for effective monitoring and notification.

Smart Water Quality Monitoring System

The Smart Water Quality Monitoring System is designed to continuously assess water quality using IoT sensors. Its primary objective is to measure parameters such as pH, temperature, and turbidity in real-time, ensuring safe and clean water. The system employs sensors integrated with an Arduino microcontroller to collect data, which is then analyzed and displayed using cloud computing. Technologies utilized include pH sensors, temperature sensors, turbidity sensors, and data visualization tools for effective monitoring and management.
